### Formulae

Formulae can be entered inline by enclosing in '$' signs, i. e. \$V := \frac{3}{4} \pi r^3\$ renders as $V := \frac{3}{4} \pi r^3$.

Or as a formula block by enclosing in double dollar signs ('\$\$'):

$$
V := \frac{3}{4} \pi r^3
$$
